Capitol City Flyers

CCF is Madison's Premier Equity Flying Club, operating out of KMSN for over 60 years. We own and operate 3 IFR-certified aircraft: a Diamond DA-40 with glass cockpit, a Cessna Skylane retractable and a Piper Archer. Come join our group of like minded pilots.

Mission:

The purpose of the Club is to promote safe, enjoyable flying and aviation-related activities, and to provide the opportunity for its members to fly aircraft economically and conveniently, supported by excellent service.
